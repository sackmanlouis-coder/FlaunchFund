05 — Earn

Earn a share of $FF trading fees.

Through Takeover.fun, the $FF Uniswap LP is split into a 10×10 grid of tiles. Hold a tile, earn your pro-rata share of the trading fees from every $FF swap — and get randomly airdropped Flaunch coins straight from the reserve.

Grid #39 · 10 × 10 Yield Live

One tile. One seat at the LP.

Every time someone trades $FF on Uniswap, the pool collects a fee. Tile holders on the Takeover grid receive their pro-rata share — automatically, for as long as they hold.

Tiles are scarce — only 100 exist per grid — and trade on an open market. More $FF volume means more fees, and every tile compounds your claim.

+ Airdrops

Tile holders are also eligible for random airdrops of Flaunch coins directly from the Flaunch Fund reserve. Hold a tile, get fees, and get surprise drops from the treasury.
